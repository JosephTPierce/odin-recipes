Tacos

Tacos are great. Everyone knows what a taco is right? I dont have to give a long drawn out description of tacos....right?

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Sauce
  • Seasoning
  • Tortillas

Steps:

  1. Brown the meat
  2. Strain the meat
  3. Add water and seasoning
  4. Let cook off
  5. Add meat, sauce and, cheese to the tortilla
  6. Enjoy!
